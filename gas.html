GAS


GAS is an abbreviation for General Academic Strand. Senior high school students are exposed to a variety of courses through the GAS, allowing them to make more informed decisions about their future career path.

It covers 15 core topics, 7 applied or contextualized topics, and 9 specialized topics.GAS provides a more generalized approach to education than focusing on one or two fields, allowing students to explore their options.

Designed to prepare students who are more interested in general areas of study rather than specialized fields, GAS will offer SHS students the option of taking electives from specialized subjects from any other strand.
