ABM


The Accountancy, Business, and Management Strand—or ABM strand is a means of analyzing a company's profitability by looking at each aspect of its business to determine strengths and weaknesses.


ABM Strand is for those who plan to take up business-related courses in higher education or engage in business, entrepreneurship, and other business-related careers.

Students who choose this path will be prepared for a variety of occupations as managers, accountants, and business owners capable of establishing extraordinary and purposeful brands capable of moving communities and economies ahead in the future.

Every successful creative brand and business necessitates top-tier talent who are business-savvy and possess managerial and financial acumen. SoFA Design Institute's unique approach to teaching the Accountancy and Business Management (ABM) strand of Senior High School (SHS) will provide aspiring creative entrepreneurs with the necessary skills to manage and run day-to-day operations of enterprises — whether their own or a multinational firm.


Subjects in ABM:

Applied Economics.

Fundamentals of Accountancy, Business and Management 1.

Fundamentals of Accountancy, Business and Management 2.
