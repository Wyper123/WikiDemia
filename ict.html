INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY(TVL-ICT)


In the TVL-ICT Strand, the students will be taking subjects that will develop the ICT Skills which will be of great help for technology-based jobs.ICT is a Senior High School strand that prepares you for a career in Information and Communication Technology. It equips you with advanced skills in computer systems, programming, creating web pages, and basic animation.


TVL-ICT is intended for students who are familiar with computers. Students in this strand also take core courses as well as specialized hands-on courses where they will be trained to use information and communication tools. The program improves students' ICT skills and competencies, which are useful for jobs in technologically advanced industries and institutions. After completing this strand, students can continue their education by pursuing degrees in fields such as web design, data management, communication, radio and television technology, graphic design, computer programming, animation, online business, IT sales, computer engineering, information technology, and software and network engineering. Animation and computer programming are two specializations available at TVL-ICT.


The ICT strand in SHS is designed to equip senior high school students with the necessary technical skills to tackle the IT industry. Graduates are expected to be able to do the following after receiving their education:


Utilize industry standard equipment and to think critically and solve problems involving software, hardware, and other digital domains

Be confident and familiar with standard tech and software necessary to carry out tasks expected of them in order to contribute meaningfully to their respective teams
